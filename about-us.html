About Us

Building Trust, One Home at a Time

Mountain View Window & Door was founded in 2006 in Centennial, CO. In February of 2012, MVWD was purchased by Devon Tilly and moved to its current location on tile row.

Our 10,000 sq. ft. facility underwent major remodeling, and we are very proud to share it with our clients. It's become a destination for homeowners and professionals alike to experience windows and doors firsthand.

All of our employees are the most experienced and qualified in their positions and are eager to provide you with the best window, door, and hardware customer service you have ever experienced.

Our Journey

Growing With Colorado

2006

The Beginning

Mountain View Window & Door was founded in Centennial, CO, with a vision to provide quality windows and doors to Colorado homeowners.

2012

New Ownership

Devon Tilly purchased MVWD in February and relocated to the current 10,000 sq. ft. facility on tile row, which underwent major remodeling.

2014

Western Expansion

Opened our second location in Grand Junction at Fairmount Hall — a historic 1922 building that served as our entry into the Western Slope community.
